    East Africa is the proposed name for the African continent's generic Top Level Domain (gTLD). It is an opportunity to forge an identity that will elevate and enhance an African digital presence...

    The Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers (ICANN), the organisation responsible for the coordination of the global Internet's systems of unique identifiers, launched a new top level domain application process in 2012. The outcome of this process will ultimately alter the landscape of the internet forever.

    The East Africa gTLD will bring the continent together as an internet community under one umbrella allowing e-commerce, technology and infrastructure to flourish. A truly African initiative created for Africa, by Africans.

    Expression Of Interest


    Sending your Expression of Interest (EOI) is quick, simple and free. While submitting an EOI does not guarantee successful registration, it is one of the smartest things you can do to obtain a successful registration. When .NGO/.ONG becomes available for registration, there will be rules and guidelines, as well as a launch sequence, which dictates who (trademark owners, early adopters, and the general public, etc.) can register when.

    • Public Interest Registry will provide an alert service containing relevant and important data pertaining to the registration process of .NGO|.ONG, such as how to register for the Trademark Clearinghouse and requirements for participation in early registration.
    • Submitting your Expression of Interest to Public Interest Registry will automatically enable you to receive developing information regarding all launch processes by the authoritative operator of the .NGO|.ONG domain extension.
      • Be among the first to learn about any offers from authorized companies handling the registration of .NGO|.ONG at launch time.
